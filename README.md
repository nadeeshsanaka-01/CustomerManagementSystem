# Customer Management System
## Introduction
In today's competitive business environment, managing customer relationships efficiently is crucial for success. The Customer Management System (CMS) is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline business operations by organizing and managing various aspects of customer engagement. This system provides a centralized platform to handle customers, products, orders, leads, tickets, and user roles, ensuring seamless tracking of customer interactions, sales, and support activities.
With an intuitive interface and robust functionality, the CMS enhances productivity by automating routine tasks, improving data accessibility, and enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions. Whether tracking customer inquiries, managing sales pipelines, or resolving support tickets, this system simplifies operations, improves response times, and fosters better customer relationships.
By integrating essential business functions into a single system, the CMS helps organizations increase efficiency, reduce manual errors, and enhance overall customer satisfaction.
## Key features
### User Management
Role-based access control (Admin, Sales, Support, Manager)
User authentication and authorization
Add, view, and delete users
### Customer Management
Add, edit, delete, and view customer profiles
Assign Points of Contact (POC)
### Product Management
Add, edit, delete, and view products
Update stock levels
### Order Management
Place and update orders
Track payment and delivery status
Review system for completed orders
### Lead Management
Add, update, and delete leads
Track follow-ups and statuses
### Ticket Management
Create, update, and track tickets
Assign tickets to support personnel
### Dashboard & Reports
Sales trend visualization using Seaborn and Streamlit
Key performance indicators (KPIs) for customers, sales, orders, and tickets
Monthly profit analysis
Order distribution by status
